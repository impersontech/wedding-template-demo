Our Story

Our love story began in the most unexpected place - a small coconut stall in the heart of Lagos. Khaleed was selling fresh coconuts, and Khadeejah stopped by to quench her thirst on a hot afternoon.

There was an instant connection, a spark that neither could ignore. She visited his shop every day, not just for the coconut water, but for the smile that lit up his face whenever she arrived.

What started as casual conversations over coconut water grew into deep discussions about dreams, family, and the future. Three years later, we're excited to begin our forever together.

We believe our meeting was destined, a perfect blend of simplicity and sweetness - much like the coconuts that brought us together.
